To verify Kirchhoff‚Äôs Voltage Law by measuring the voltages across each resistor in a closed loop and proving that the algebraic sum of voltages is zero.

### **Objectives**
- To apply KVL in a closed electrical loop.  
- To measure voltage across each resistor.  
- To compare theoretical and practical values.  
- To understand the real-time application of Kirchhoff‚Äôs Voltage Law.

### **Procedure**
1. Make circuit connections by dragging nodes in the simulation.
2. Connect power supply (1‚Äì9, 2‚Äì10).
3. Connect voltmeters across the resistors.  
4. Click **CHECK** to validate connections.
5. Set R1, R2, R3 values.
6. Turn **Power ON**.
7. Adjust voltage slider and observe voltmeter readings.
8. Click **ADD** to store readings.
